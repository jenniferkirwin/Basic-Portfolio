About Me

Hello, my name is Jen. I am a marketer turned web developer, living in New Hampshire and making embroidery art in my spare time.

At the start of my career I was a self-taught front-end developer, building websites from scratch with HTML and CSS and managing AdWords and digital marketing campaigns for a marketing agency. When I moved to Boston I landed a marketing job. Over the next 3 years of marketing, the companies I worked for underwent website redesigns and database updates. I saw what the developers were creating and said to myself, “this is good, but I can make an even better website”. When my husband and I moved back to New Hampshire recently, I decided to pursue web development once again.

To help jumpstart myself, I am taking a class to better understand back-end development and build my portfolio once again. Check out my latest projects here! Want to chat? Feel free to contact me at any time! Like art? Check out my embroideries at www.jenniferlamontagne.com.
